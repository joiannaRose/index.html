Soy Candles


One of the most enjoyable activities on a relaxing afternoon is making something you can use! Make a candle with someone you love!

HomeMade Candle Kits have everything you need to do Self-Care the Natural Way


We recommend using repurposed containers for this project! Our favorites are 4oz -8oz glass jars


Supply List

  • 16oz. Soy Flakes
  • .05oz essential oil blend
  • 3oz beezwax
  • 10cm wick included (trim to fit container height)
  • melting pot
  • 2 or 3 small to medium jars
  • spatula
  • wick stablizers

Instructions

  1. Put Beeswax flakes in cookware
  2. Slowly heat to 130degrees Fahrenheit or 30 degrees Celsius
  3. Add Soy Flakes
  4. After wax begins to melt, add essential oils
  5. Keep on low heat, be careful not to boil
  6. As wax melts, prep jars
  7. Use a small amount of wax to affix wick base to bottom of container
  8. Use included wick stablizer to position wick near the center of smaller containers
  9. After wick is set, gradually pour wax mixture into containers
  10. Adjust wick as needed
  11. Let cool until wax hardens
  12. Enjoy your home-made ambiance!
